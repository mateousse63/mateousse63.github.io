Privacy Policy

Learn more: https://policies.google.com/privacy

The app uses Google Firebase for analytics and crash reporting. Firebase may collect anonymous usage data, device information, and crash logs to improve app performance.

In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed securely through Google Play. Google may collect transaction-related information in accordance with its privacy policy.

Data Usage

The information collected by third-party services is used to improve the app, provide advertising, and maintain functionality.

Your Choices

You can limit ad personalization through your device settings. You may also uninstall the app to stop data collection.
